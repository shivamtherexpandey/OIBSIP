About Karyakaram

Karyakaram is an event scheduling platform that simplifies event discovery and management. With a user-friendly interface, it offers a wide range of events, from conferences and workshops to music concerts and cultural festivals. Users can browse through upcoming events, access event details, and conveniently schedule attendance. Whether you're an event organizer or an attendee, Karyakaram is the go-to platform to stay informed about exciting events and effectively plan your calendar.
